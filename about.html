About Aqua Zen

Our Story

At Aqua Zen, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the benefits of yoga, aquatics, and cycling. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and supportive environment where people of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds can come together to improve their physical and mental health. We offer a variety of classes to meet the needs of all our members, and our experienced instructors are passionate about helping people reach their goals.
Aqua Zen was founded in 2020 by Lance Arrow, a certified yoga instructor and personal trainer. Lance was inspired to start the studio after seeing the positive impact that yoga had on his own life. He wanted to create a space where people could come to relax, rejuvenate, and build community.
Aqua Zen has since grown into a thriving community of yoga, aquatics, and cycling enthusiasts. We offer a variety of classes for all levels, and our instructors are passionate about helping people reach their goals. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the benefits of these activities, and we are committed to providing a welcoming and supportive environment where people of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds can come together to improve their physical and mental health.
In addition to yoga, aquatics, and cycling classes, Aqua Zen also offers a variety of other services, including:
* Personal Training
* Nutrition Counseling
* Massage Therapy
* Reiki
* Meditation classes
We believe that a holistic approach to health is the best way to achieve optimal well-being. We offer a variety of services to help our members reach their goals, both physically and mentally.
We are committed to providing our members with the best possible experience. We offer a clean and modern facility, friendly and knowledgeable staff, and a variety of classes to choose from. We are confident that you will find what you are looking for at Aqua Zen.
